The Point

Part joke, part memory aid, part oddly elegant dog-life archive.

Pooppy started from a very real dog-owner problem: when multiple people walk the same dog, everybody wants to know whether the business has already been handled.

So instead of building yet another joyless tracker, Pooppy treats each logged poop like a tiny field report: where it happened, what the weather was doing, how dramatic the conditions were, and whether the dog deserves applause.

Inside The App

Absurdly overqualified poop intelligence.
